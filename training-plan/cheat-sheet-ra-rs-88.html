üëë The Golden Rule

Fluidity + >80% Content

To hit 88, you need the correct sequence of words. 50% isn't enough anymore. Aim for almost perfect phrases with exact pitch matching.

1. The "Echo Chamber"

Do not translate meaning instantly. Hold the acoustic sound in your head.

Trust the Echo.

You have 2-3 seconds of raw audio memory. Replay the sound mentally before speaking.

2. The 80% Threshold

Long sentence? You must capture more than just the start.

Input: "The assignment must be submitted by Friday noon."
79 Score: "The assignment must be submitted." 88 Score: "The assignment must be submitted by Friday."

Sacrifice the last word if needed, but get the bulk of the object phrase.

3. Shadowing Stress

If the speaker stresses "URGENT", you stress "URGENT". Mimic the emotional state, not just the words.

Day 1 Protocol (88+ Intensity)

1. Warm Up (Academic) Do not read news. Read Scientific Abstracts aloud. Get used to academic vocabulary and sentence structures.
2. Distraction Training Turn the TV volume UP. You must be able to focus on the 'Echo' while ignoring background noise completely.
3. The "Robot Check" Record yourself. Do you sound bored? If yes, you fail. You must sound interested and authoritative.
