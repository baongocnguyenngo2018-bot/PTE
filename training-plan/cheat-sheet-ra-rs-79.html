RS Repeat Sentence

üëë The Golden Rule

Fluency > Content

50% of the words spoken smoothly scores HIGHER than 100% of the words spoken with hesitation.

1. The Emergency Stop

You forgot the second half of the sentence. What do you do?

STOP SPEAKING.

Say what you remember clearly, then go silent and click Next. Do not say "umm" or try to guess. The silence is safe; hesitation is fatal.

2. The "First Half" Focus

Long sentence? Ignore the end intentionally.

Input: "The library will be closed for renovation until next Monday afternoon."
Output: "The library will be closed for renovation."

This is enough for a strong score if the fluency is perfect.

3. Mimicry Mode

Copy the speaker's emotion. If they sound angry, you sound angry. If they speak fast, you speak fast. The AI matches sound waves, not just words.

Day 1 Drill Protocol

1. Warm Up (5 mins) Read a random news article out loud at 120% speed. Get your tongue moving. Do tongue twisters.
2. The "Bad Mic" Simulation Practice with some background noise (TV or cafe sounds) to simulate the exam center distraction.
3. Review Waveforms Look at your recording waveform. Do you see gaps/silence? Eliminating those gaps is your #1 goal today.
