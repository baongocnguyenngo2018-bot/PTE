Master the Tasks: Tips & Tricks

Read Aloud

  • One-Breath Rule: Pause >3s = mic off.
  • Intonation: Lower pitch at full stops.
  • Never Correct: If you stumble, KEEP GOING. Fluency > Accuracy.

Repeat Sentence

  • First 50% Rule: Nail the first half perfectly if the sentence is too long.
  • Mimic Tone: Copy their anger, happiness, or speed.
  • No hesitation: Speak immediately.

Describe Image

"The image shows [Title]. The highest is [X], the lowest is [Y]. In conclusion..."
  • Don't Think: Just read the title and 2 numbers.
  • Panic Phrase: "The image also reveals max & min values."

Retell Lecture

  • Notes: Write 5-6 key phrases (Subj + Verb). No full sentences.
  • Connector Template: "He mentioned [A]. Furthermore, he discussed [B]. In conclusion [C]."
NEW

Respond to Situation

Roleplay: Be polite but firm.

1. Acknowledge: "I understand your frustration..."
2. Solve: "The reason is [X]. However, I can [Y]."
3. Close: "Thank you for your patience."
NEW

Summarize Group Disc.

Track who agrees vs. who disagrees.

  • Structure: "Speaker A suggested [X], whereas Speaker B argued [Y]."
  • Timing: Aim for 60-90 seconds. Don't ramble for the full 2 mins if you are empty.

12-Day Intensive Plan

1

Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-4)

Day 1 Read Aloud & Repeat Sentence

Record 20 RAs. Do 30 RSs (focus on fluency).

Day 2 Templates (DI)

Memorize 1 Universal Template. Apply to 10 graphs.

Day 3 Retell Lecture

Listen to TED talks (60s). Practice note-taking keywords.

Day 4 New Tasks

Simulate 5 'Situations'. Practice tone variation.

2

Phase 2: Speed & Intensity (Days 5-9)

Day 5
High-Speed RA
Day 6
DI Marathon (20 in a row)
Day 7
RS Memory Training
Day 8 Integrated Speaking

Alternate RL and Group Discussion to switch brain modes.

Day 9 Rapid Fire

50 Short Answer Qs + 10 Situation Drills.

3

Phase 3: Simulation (Days 10-12)

Day 10 Full Mock Test With background noise.
Day 11 Review Weakness Fix the one task dragging you down.
Day 12 Rest & Warmup Light practice. No new vocab.

Strategy: 2 Hours/Day (1 Hr Speaking + 1 Hr Integrated)
